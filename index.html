DCRUST SOCIETIES

Introduction



Get a chance to explore yourself through the wide varities of socities present at DCRUST.




Socities

Thinkbots


The technical society where you can learn anything varying from machine learning to web development. Here you can sharpen your coding skills or work on robots and AI.


Lisoc

The Literary Society or Lisoc is for the enthusiasts who love to read or write or speak or both! Lisoc provides you a platform to showcase your literature skills and also helps to build up your personality.


Goonj


Goonj is the only musical society at DCRUST. They have performed and achieved excellence at various inter college events such as Rendezvous(IIT'D cultural fest), Antaragini(IIT Kanpur cultural fest) and so on.


Dramatics

SKA a society full of energy just like its members and JZAA the all rounders group are the two dramatics club where students can bring a new personality out of themselves.


Dance Groups


If you love western go REBEL and if you have a punjabi swag then RPG vs NPG will always be there. The dance groups are always actively participating in all events and are mostly in popular demand. If you have the skill, show it off!


IIC

Institute Innovation Council provides students and teachers scholarships, mentorship programmes and workshops relating to Enterpreneurship. It gives guidance to the students and helps in building careers by funding start ups.


Savera


While walking through the campus you'll get to see a lot of unprivelged kids and to help them out Dcrust had taken an initiative for the education of these children which is now popularly known by the residents as 'Savera School'. Any student can volunteer to teach here children from classes 1 to 12.
